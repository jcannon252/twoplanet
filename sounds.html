THE SOUNDS OF THE PLANET

This futurist planet has cities that contribute to the success of the planet.The buildings are clad in
the native planets, the animals live in harmony amongst the people. The technology is advanced.
As with any good city the solitude of nature is only miles away; and the people value both equally. Birds and bugs create
a background noise that is calming and serene. The weather patterns omit a frequent soft hum. When not on the planet Jadzia
travels with a modest crew where the design of their spaceship is modeled after their home. The center of the ship
is their enviromental deck, a complete replica of the gorgeous and giving forest. Back on their world the planet glows in
the bask of two suns, the days are long and and warm. The ancient memebers of their society valued living in harmony
with the land while still advancing their technology; the people pay homage to nature once a year in a great festival.
