Two Suns City

Because of the light from the two suns,the planet has a rich and vibrant wildlife. Plants are incorporated into everything;
buildings, spaceships, technology, culture. The people honor nature and prioritize it's importance.
There is a great balance between remote nature and city life,
and the infrastructure provides easy access between both. The mountains are opalescent,
the ocean and rivers turquoise. It is a magic planet and the people unique.
