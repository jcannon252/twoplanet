Jadzia & Her Home


Born on the outskirts of the Andromeda Galaxy, on a planet with two suns,
lives a person named Jadzia. She is tall, curious, her hair and skin are normally shades of purple and blue.
However, because the people of the planet change colors based on their moods, when Jadzia is angry, she turns red, when she is overjoyed she turns yellow.
Jadzia spends her time exploring space and traveling to other plants, but always returns home.
